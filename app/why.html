About vanfringe-browser

This application allows you to browse upcoming shows at the vancouver fringe in a table. It is intended to help you find shows to see. Shows that occur after this year's fringe or before the current date are not displayed.

The app has several features. You can sort by columns by clicking the header. You can sort by multiple columns with the SHIFT key. You can search for events. You can hide and show columns. You can select rows by clicking and with SHIFT and COMMNAD/CTRL.You can export your current table to PDF or Excel format.
